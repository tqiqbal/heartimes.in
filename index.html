Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about hearing health and our services

If you experience difficulty understanding conversations, frequently ask people to repeat themselves, or need to turn up the TV volume louder than others prefer, these could be signs of hearing loss. Our audiologists in Delhi-NCR can perform a comprehensive hearing test to determine if you might benefit from hearing aids.

At HearTimes Clinic in Gurugram and Delhi-NCR, we offer a wide range of hearing aid options including behind-the-ear (BTE), in-the-ear (ITE), in-the-canal (ITC), completely-in-canal (CIC), and invisible-in-canal (IIC) styles. We also provide the latest digital and smart hearing aids with Bluetooth connectivity and smartphone control features.

Hearing aid prices vary depending on the technology level, features, and style. At our audiology clinic, we offer options to fit various budgets, starting from basic models to premium devices with advanced features. We also provide flexible payment plans and will help you navigate any insurance coverage you may have.

With proper care and maintenance, most hearing aids last between 5-7 years. Our clinic offers regular maintenance services to ensure your devices remain in optimal condition. We also provide cleaning kits and instructions for daily care to maximize the lifespan of your hearing aids.
